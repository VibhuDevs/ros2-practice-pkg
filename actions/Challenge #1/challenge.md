This was a practice challenge session based on ROS2 actions concept.
The challenge was based on a very real world practical application of a robot that charges itself upto a particular target percentage and quits charging automatically once it has reached the desired level of charging. This eliminates the need for manual charging a robot. User can also cancel charging midway forcibly and see the current charge that the robot has. 

Future applications could be a robot that feels the need to charge itself subjected to a few conditions and auto navigates it's way to the charging station and charges itself.  

I was supposed to add the following:
1. A goal sending and accepting mechanism
2. A response mechanism
3. A cancel mechanism
